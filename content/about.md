# About Us — Connecting & Protecting Rural Communities

## Global Expertise, Local Mission

Camfor is founded by Gerard, who brings decades of experience from leading significant connectivity projects worldwide to the heart of the Macedon Ranges.

After years of contributing to global advancements in digital infrastructure, Gerard sought a more meaningful impact closer to home. Settling in the Macedon Ranges and starting a hobby farm, he witnessed firsthand the connectivity challenges faced by rural communities.

Driven by a desire to apply his extensive expertise where it's most needed, Gerard founded Camfor. Our goal is straightforward: to provide rural and urban Victoria with access to reliable, high-speed internet. Transitioning from international projects to focusing on local needs, Gerard's journey underpins Camfor's commitment to enhancing digital accessibility for all.

Through Camfor, we're turning Gerard's vision into reality, ensuring no community is left behind in today's digital world.
